CHOCOLATE CAKE

DESCRIPTION

Indulge in the rich goodness of our chocolate cake, As the cake's intense chocolate aroma fills the air, the smooth, creamy frosting adds a luxurious touch, perfectly complementing the cake's dense, fudge texture. With each successive bite, the flavors meld together in perfect harmony, a true delight for the senses. Whether your'e simply seeking a sweet treat, this sumptuous cake is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

INGREDIENTS

  • For the Cake:
  • 23/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 11/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs at room temperature

  • For the chocolate buttercream frosting (optional):
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 cups confectioner's sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 ounce high quality dark or semisweet chocolate, melted

  • Additional ingredients:
  • Vegetable oil for greasing the pan
  • confectioner's sugar for dusting (optional).

STEPS:

  • Preheat and prepare the pans:
    •

    1. Preheat your oven to 350oF (175oC)
    2. Grease two 9-inch (23cm) round cake pans and line the bottoms with parchment paper

  • Mix the Dry Ingredients:
    •

    1. in a medium bowl, whisk together:
      • Flour
      • Sugar
      • Baking Powder
      • Salt
      • Cocoa Powder

  • Mix the Wet ingredients:
    •

    1. in a large mixing bowl, whisk together:
      • Milk
      • Eggs
      • Vanilla Extract

  • Combine the wet and dry ingredients:
    •

    1. Gradually pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
    2. Beat the mixture for 2-3 minutes until well combined

  • Divide and pour the Batter:
    •

    1. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans
    2. Smooth th tops with spatula.

  • Bake the Cakes
    •

    1. Bake for 30-35minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
    2. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pans for 10 minutes.

  • Cool and Frost the Cakes (optional)
    •

    1. Transfer the cakes to a wire rack to cool completely
    2. if desired, frost with chocolate buttercream frosting (recipe below)

  • Chocolate Buttercream Frosting: (optional)
    •

    1. Beat the butter and confectioner's sugar until Smooth
    2. Add vanilla extract, cocoa powder and melted chocolate. Beat until combined.
    3. Adjust the frosting consistency and flavor as needed.

  • Assemble and Serve:
    •

    1. Once the cakes are completely cool, place one layer on a serving plate
    2. Spread a layer of frosting on top (if using)
    3. Place the second layer on top and frost entire cake (if using)
    4. Slice and serve
