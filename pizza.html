PIZZA MARGARITAS

DESCRIPTION

Pizza Margaritas is a classic Neapolitan-style pizza that embodies the simplicity and flavor of italian cuisine. The thin crust, hand-kneaded and wood fired, provides a crispy exterior and soft interior, setting the stage for the delicate balance of toppings. Fresh mozarella cheese, sliced into thin rounds, adds creaminess and subtle sweetened, while the tangy tomato sauce, made from San Marzano tomatoes, brings a touch of acidity.

The finishing touch is a sprinkle of fresh basil leaves, adding a pop color and a bright, herbaceous flavor. As the flavors meld together, the initial crunch of the crust gives way to the softness of the cheese and sauce, followed by the subtle herbal note of the basil. With each bite, the harmony of flavors and textures transports you to the sun-kissed hills of italy, making Pizza Margherita a true culinary delight.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg flour (preferably Caput or All purpose flour).
  • 625 ml water.
  • 10g active dry yeast.
  • 20g salt.
  • 10g extra-virgin olive oil.
  • 250g San Marzano tomatoes (hand crushed).
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Salt, to taste.
  • 8 ounces (225g) mozzarella cheese, sliced.
  • Fresh basil leaves.
  • Extra-virgin olive oil for brushing the crust (optional).
  • Salt for sprinkling on crust (optional).

STEPS:

  • Make the dough:

    1. in a mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast and salt.
    2. Gradually add water, mixing until a dough forms.
    3. Add olive oil and knead for 10-15 minutes.
    4. Let the dough rest at room temperature for 1-2hours.

  • Prepare the Sauce:

    1. Crush the San Marzano tomatoes by hand.
    2. Mix with garlic, olive oil and salt.

  • Shape the dough:

    1. Divide the dough into 2-3 equal portions.
    2. Roll out each portion into a thin circle (12-14 inches).

  • Top the pizza:

    1. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce.
    2. Arrange sliced Mozzarella Cheese.
    3. Add fresh Basil leaves.

  • Bake the Pizza:

    1. preheat oven to 425oF (220oC).
    2. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden brown.

  • Finish and serve:

    1. Remove from the oven and let cool for 2-3 minutes.
    2. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil (optional).
    3. Sprinkle with salt (optional).
    4. Slice and serve hot.
