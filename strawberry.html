STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE

DESCRIPTION

Strawberry shortcake a tender shortcake filled with sweetened fresh strawberries and topped with whipped cream or vanilla ice-cream, creating a classic dessert with a delightful mix of textures and flavors making it a perfect dessert for warm weather, picnics or any occasion.

INGREDIENTS

  • For the Shortcake:
  • 11/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of granulated Sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream or buttermilk

  • For the Strawberries
  • 2 cups of sliced fresh strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

  • For the Whipped Cream:
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

STEPS:

  • Shortcake:

    1. Preheat oven to 375oF (190oC)
    2. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
    3. Add cold butter and mix until crumbly
    4. pour in cream or buttermilk and stir until dough forms
    5. Roll out dough and cut into rounds
    6. Bake for 18-20minutes or until lightly golden

  • Strawberries

    1. Slice Fresh strawberries and mix with granulated sugar
    2. Let it sit for about 15-20 minutes allowing strawberries to release their juice

  • Assembling:

    1. Split shortcake rounds in half horizontally.
    2. Spoon sweetened strawberries over the bottom half
    3. Top with the shortcake's top half
    4. Dollop whipped cream or scoop vanilla ice cream top.

  • Whipped Cream:

    1. Whip heavy cream and granulated sugar until stiff peaks form
